ANTHEM (2019) dir. Priya Jeram-Patel

This was my senior project at UCT. My role was editor.

In 1994, Archbishop Desmond Tutu described South Africa as a "rainbow nation." What does it mean to be a rainbow nation? We sought to explore this question in ANTHEM (2019) as well as the concept of nationhood in general. The film features one South African from a diverse background for each verse of the national anthem. Each subject confronts their personhood through the words of the song.

South African Film and Television Awards 14 - Best Student Film Nominee

University of Cape Town Screen Production Graduate Film 2019

QUEER (2019) dir. Priya Jeram-Patel

QUEER (2019) is divided into five segments, each featuring one self-indentifying LGBTQ+ youth. We gave each of the subjects full creative control of their segments - from direction to production. The result is a film that exemplifies the diversity and individuality of the LGBTQ+ community.

Screened at Shnit International Short Film Festival 2019.
