ANTHEM (2019) dir. Priya Jeram-Patel

This was my senior project at UCT. My role was editor.

We wanted to explore the concept of nationhood and the concept of a true rainbow, so we got four South Africans from diverse backgrounds to confront their personhood through the verses of the South African national anthem.

South African Film and Television Awards 14 - Best Student Film Nominee

University of Cape Town Screen Production Graduate Film 2019

QUEER (2019) dir. Priya Jeram-Patel

An experimental film about queer individuals. The idea was to give creative power to five self-indentifying LGBTQ+ youths. From concept to edit, this was experimental. Each individual had their unique to flare to their 'sections' of the video.

Screened at Shnit International Short Film Festival 2019.
